Grand Prize

One winning reel will receive the grand award for impact, creativity, and message clarity.

How to Participate

  1. Follow our Instagram page: @sukhisevafoundation
  2. Create a 15–60s reel that promotes kindness, helping others, or positive community action.
  3. Post your reel on Instagram with the hashtag #SukhiReels2025 and tag @sukhisevafoundation.
  4. Add a caption about your idea and how it makes a difference. Encourage others to donate at sukhingo.org/donate.
  5. Ensure your profile or the post visibility is public so judges can view your entry.

Suggested Caption (copy & use)

Tip: Use clear audio, strong lighting, and a short, powerful message. Show real actions or ideas people can follow.

Rules & Eligibility

  • Original content only; no copyrighted music/images unless you have rights.
  • Reel duration: 15–60 seconds. Post must be public (or the reel shareable).
  • Include #SukhiReels2025 and tag @sukhisevafoundation in the caption.
  • Winners are chosen by Sukhi NGO panel based on impact, clarity, creativity, and reach.
  • Decision of the jury is final. Prize is ₹5,00,000 (INR) to one winner.
  • By participating, you allow Sukhi NGO to repost your reel with credit.

Note: Timelines, result dates, and any additional terms will be announced on our Instagram page.
